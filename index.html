> Our services

My approach to recruitment is partnership based. We will treat your business as if it was our own.

As a business, I can find you the best talent. If you work in technology, I can help find your perfect job.

Clients

I’ll be honest about how the search is going, what people say about your company and our next steps. By working in a partnership I’ll be on the lookout for someone who could make a real difference to your business. I focus my efforts on meeting people, networking and selling your business.

Candidates

You might not feel ready to look and you might not hate your job just yet. Or maybe you do hate your job. We can work actively or passively together - put a plan in place of who you’d like to work for and I’ll do the hunting, or we can simply wait for a suitable role to come up, if you’re not in a hurry.
